How Credit Cards Work (Canada)

Credit cards are a convenient way to borrow short-term funds from banks or issuers like Visa, Mastercard, and American Express.

01

Credit Limit

Your credit limit depends on your income and credit history.

02

Pay your balance

Pay your balance in full each month to avoid high interest charges.

03

Minimum Payments

Minimum payments will keep your account in good standing, but you'll pay significant interest over time.
