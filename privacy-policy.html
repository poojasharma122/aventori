Privacy Policy

At Aventori, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect two types of information:

  • Personal Information You Provide:
    • Name, email address, or other contact details (when you submit a contact form or subscribe to a newsletter).
    • Any information you voluntarily share with us via email or feedback forms
  • Non-Identifiable / Technical Information:
    • IP address, browser type, device type, pages you visit, and time spent on our site.
    • Cookies or tracking technologies to help improve site performance and user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To improve our website, tools, and recommendations.
  • To communicate with you if you submit a request or inquiry.
  • To analyze site traffic and user behaviour for optimization.
  • To display relevant credit card comparisons and offers.
  • To comply with legal or regulatory requirements.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our site uses cookies and analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Measure traffic and performance.
  • Display relevant ads or retargeting campaigns.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some site features may not function properly.

4. Third-Party Links

Aventori contains links to third-party websites, including bank and credit card provider sites.

  • We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites.
  • Review their privacy policies before sharing personal information.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share limited data with trusted partners or service providers (e.g., analytics, hosting) only when necessary to operate our services.

In rare cases, we may disclose information to comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests

6. Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored securely on servers with appropriate technical and administrative safeguards.

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed 100% secure.

7. Retention of Data

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy or as required by law.

Once no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize your data.
